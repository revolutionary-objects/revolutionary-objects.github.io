About me

Hello! I'm Holly

I'm a student of Chinese and international history. I've lived in Beijing and Xinjiang, and I speak Mandarin Chinese. My master's dissertation researches the international circulation of Chinese propaganda posters during the early 1970s.


I built Revolutionary objects to showcase my collection of Chinese 20th century material objects. It's my first website.
